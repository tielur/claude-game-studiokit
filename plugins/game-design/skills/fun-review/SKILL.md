Evaluate the user's game design or mechanic for fun. Read the framework in `references/framework.md` for the full evaluation model, and check `references/gotchas.md` for common analysis mistakes to avoid.

The core idea: fun is not juice — it's whether the player has a reason to keep engaging. Focus your evaluation on incentives, tension, aesthetics, surprise, and clarity.

For each relevant dimension, assess what's working and what's not. Be direct and specific. End with the 1-2 highest-leverage changes.

Judge the design on its own terms. Not every game needs every dimension.
